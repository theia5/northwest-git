Recycling Information

We are now able to start receiving concrete and bituminous again in our yard. No dumping fee at this time. Contact our office in advance if you will be dumping more than 10 loads on any given day so we can have equipment available to keep the pile open. Watch our website for future updates. Dates are only approximate. Contact Northwest for any of your future recycle needs. Quotes to be given on an individual basis.

    Conditions of Recycle Purchase
  • (1) day advance notice to supply material.
  • 1,000 tons minimum order per day to justify keeping a loader and operator available to load.
    • Northwest loader/scale only.
    • There will be a surcharge for the cost of the loader on days less thean 1,000 ton @ $1.35/ton for difference in tons taken to 1,000 ton minimum
  • Quantity not guaranteed.
  • Future quotes will be provided on a per job basis and will be dependent on availability of material.
  • Upon signed agreement with Northwest, we will make every effort to retain and provide the tonnage we have available at the time of our agreement.
  • Price is good for current year or while material is available.
  • Customer to confirm material meets their requirements prior to agreement.
  • Payment for material within 30 days of invoice.
